## 3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

**We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the FTC's 2025 amendments.**

### 3.1 Parental Consent Requirements

**For users under 13 years old:**
- We require verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information
- Parents must create and manage accounts on behalf of children under 13
- We use FTC-approved consent methods including:
  - Credit card verification (small authorization charge)
  - Signed consent form via email
  - Video call verification
  - Knowledge-based authentication

### 3.2 Information Collected from Children

We collect only information reasonably necessary to provide our services:
- First name or nickname (not full name without parental consent)
- Age/date of birth
- Parent/guardian email for consent
- Sport and training preferences
- Performance data (assessment scores, workout completions)

**We do NOT collect from children under 13:**
- Precise geolocation
- Photos or videos without parental consent
- Social media identifiers
- Biometric data

### 3.3 Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:
- **Review:** Request a description of information collected from their child
- **Access:** Receive a copy of their child's personal information
- **Delete:** Request deletion of their child's information
- **Revoke Consent:** Withdraw consent and terminate the account
- **Limit Collection:** Restrict collection to only what's necessary for participation

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@youthperformance.com

### 3.4 Teen Privacy (Ages 13-17)

For users ages 13-17:
- Account creation requires parental acknowledgment
- Parents retain access to account activity
- Teens can manage certain privacy settings with parental oversight
- We do not sell teen data or use it for behavioral advertising

### 3.5 School and Team Settings

When athletes access YP through a school or team:
- The school/team administrator acts as the parent's agent for consent purposes
- We do not use student data for commercial purposes beyond the educational service
- Data is governed by agreements with the school/team that comply with FERPA (where applicable)

**YouthPerformance Academy does not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.**

We share information with trusted vendors who help us operate the Platform:

### 4.3 When Required by Law

We may disclose information:
- To comply with legal obligations
- In response to valid legal process (subpoenas, court orders)

- In emergencies involving potential threats to safety

### 4.4 With Your Consent

We may share information when you direct us to:
- Coach/team data sharing (when you join a team)
- Third-party app integrations you authorize
- Public profile information you choose to share

### 4.5 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will provide notice and the opportunity to delete your data before any transfer.

### 4.6 Aggregated and De-identified Data

We may share aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify any individual:
- Youth athletic benchmarks and norms
- Research publications on athletic development
- Industry reports and statistics

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:
- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 for data in transit; AES-256 for data at rest
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication for staff

### 7.2 Your Responsibilities

You can help protect your account by:
