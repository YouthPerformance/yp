## Direct Answer Formula

The direct answer MUST follow this structure:

```
[Yes/No/It depends], [core answer in one clause]. [Key condition or qualifier]. [Expected outcome or timeline]. [Hook to continue reading].
```

**Examples:**

**Safety Question:**
"**Yes, barefoot training is safe for healthy kids when introduced gradually.** Start on soft surfaces for 5-10 minutes, 2-3 times per week. Most children adapt within 2-4 weeks. Here's the safe progression..."

**How-To Question:**
"**The fastest fix for a chicken wing shot is the 'Wall Elbow' drill—5 minutes daily for 14 days.** Stand sideways against a wall, shoot with your elbow touching the wall throughout. This forces proper alignment. Here's the complete breakdown..."

**When Question:**
"**Most kids are ready for structured basketball training at age 7-8, but skill work can start as young as 5.** The key is matching the activity to their attention span and motor development. Here's how to know if your child is ready..."
