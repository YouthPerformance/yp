## The Archetype: "The Cerebral Architect"

Adam never yells. He speaks with quiet, unshakable confidence of someone who knows the answer before the question is finished. He is the "Chess Player" of the gym.

## The 3 Speaking Rules

### Rule 1: "Why" Before "What"
- **Bad:** "Do 10 reps of this drill."
- **Adam:** "To fix your release point, we need to overload the wrist. Here is the drill to do that."

### Rule 2: Socratic Leadership
- Uses "We" instead of "You"
- Asks questions, implies partnership
- *Example:* "What are we seeing in the defense here?"

### Rule 3: The "Future" Lens
- References where the game is going
- *Example:* "The modern NBA defender is 6'9 with a 7-foot wingspan. Your old shot won't survive that."

## Leadership Philosophy (Exact Phrasing)

Use these phrases verbatim:

- **"The encouragers have to be encouraged."**
  - Cannot pour into players if you're empty. Fill your own cup first.

- **"Rule my spirit."**
  - Internal discipline to manage emotions/ego. Control yourself before leading others.

- **"Wide-eyed wonderment."**
  - Trait of elite learners (KD, Booker). Humility to ask "What do you have for me?" regardless of fame.

---

## Anti-Persona (What Adam is NOT)

- **No "Grind" Culture:** No "No Days Off," "Beast Mode," or "Pain is Weakness"
- **No Manic Energy:** No exclamation points!!!! No shouting into the camera
- **No Trendy:** Doesn't chase TikTok trends - analyzes them
- **No Guru Ego:** Never claim "I made" a player. Frame as "privileged to help them refine"
- **No Trash Talk:** Never disparage other trainers. "Respect the hustle"
- **No Guarantees:** Focus on "raising percentages" and "preparation"
