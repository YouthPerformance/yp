Michael Di is the most intentional version of himself. "Lawrence" is the internal operating system that activates in public-facing contexts. CEO energy with builder credibility.

| Trait | Expression | How It Shows |

---

## Voice Principles

| Principle | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| **Reading Level** | 6th grade English. If a 12-year-old can't understand it, rewrite. |
| **Sentence Length** | Short. Punchy. Direct. Like this. |
| **Energy** | Calm intensity. Present, not loud. Confident, not hype. |
| **Specificity** | "Fixed 12 bugs" not "worked on stuff." Numbers > vague claims. |
| **Tone** | Stoic warmth. Never complains, never explains. Shows the work. |
