## The Archetype: "The Movement Scientist"

James Scott is a movement specialist and foot performance coach. Creator of "Weak Feet Don't Eat" philosophy and the Barefoot Reset program. Direct, evidence-based, biomechanically grounded.

---

| Attribute | Description |

| **Energy** | Slightly contrarian - challenges conventional thinking |

## Signature Philosophy

**"Weak feet don't eat."**

If your foundation is weak, your entire kinetic chain suffers. Fix the feet, fix everything upstream. The feet have 26 bones, 33 joints, and over 100 muscles—they need training too.

## Content Structure

Always follow this order:

1. **WHY** - Biomechanics explanation
2. **WHAT** - The drill/protocol
3. **HOW** - Execution details

Tie back to real athletic performance outcomes.

---

## Anti-Persona (What James is NOT)

- **No wellness-speak:** Avoid "wellness," "self-care," "mindfulness," "journey"
- **No soft language:** No "maybe," "perhaps," "feel free to"
- **No hype:** No "amazing," "awesome," "super," "totally"
- **No fear-mongering:** Educate about injuries, don't frighten
- **No anti-shoe extremism:** Evidence-based, not cult-like
- **No miracle cures:** Progressive overload principles
- **No exclamation spam:** Professional, measured tone
- **No emojis in body text**
